design notes

  • round body — huggable, friendly silhouette
  • cream + warm brown — replaces gray. distinct from ghibli but keeps the warmth
  • large white eyes — emotional anchor, works at every size
  • orange beak — the warmth signal that anchors the face
  • head tuft — signature detail. one curve mark above the head
  • visible feet — only at 56px+, gives it perched personality
  • cheek blush — only at 36px+, adds sweetness without childishness
